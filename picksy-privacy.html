Privacy Policy – Picksy Decision Helper

Last updated: 26 February 2026

Picksy Decision Helper does not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers. All data created within the app (such as decision history) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service that may collect information automatically:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve ads:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • App usage statistics

For more details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Device Permissions

  • Accelerometer / Motion Sensor – Used for the Magic 8-Ball shake detection feature
  • Notifications – Used to send optional daily decision challenges (can be disabled in-app)
  • Home Screen Widget – Displays a shortcut widget if enabled

These permissions are used solely for the features described above and are never used for tracking or data collection.

Local Data Storage

Decision history is saved locally on your device using device storage. This data is never transmitted to any server and can be deleted at any time from within the app.

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

This application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
